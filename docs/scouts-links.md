# Scout Association Links

## Systems and tools

### [Scouts.org.uk](https://www.scouts.org.uk)
The homepage of The Scout Association — a gateway to the various digital systems, frameworks, and resource assets available.

### [My Membership](https://membership.scouts.org.uk)
The central membership system managing individual volunteer profiles, personal details, background disclosures, training credits, and roles.

### [My Learning](https://learn.scouts.org.uk/)
The digital training platform to complete mandatory compliance modules, ongoing learning paths, and optional volunteer development courses.

### [Digital Skills Courses](https://digitalskills.scouts.org.uk/)
A curated hub of interactive guides, toolkits, and short courses designed to improve IT literacy and help navigate online Scouting software.

### [Policy, Organisation & Rules (POR)](https://www.scouts.org.uk/por/)
The core governance rules, structures, and operational boundaries of UK Scouting. Regularly updated to ensure safe and authorized local practice.

## Information Cards

### [The Yellow Card](https://www.scouts.org.uk/volunteers/staying-safe-and-safeguarding/safe-scouting-cards/safeguarding-code-of-conduct-for-adults-yellow-card/)
The official Safeguarding Code of Conduct for all adult volunteers, outlining clear rules for keeping young people safe.

### [The Purple Card](https://www.scouts.org.uk/volunteers/staying-safe-and-safeguarding/safe-scouting-cards/safe-scouting-what-to-do-in-an-emergency/)
The crisis management template detailing the exact steps, protocols, and notification chains to follow during an emergency.

### [The Orange Card](https://www.scouts.org.uk/volunteers/staying-safe-and-safeguarding/safe-scouting-cards/safeguarding-code-of-conduct-for-young-leaders-orange-card/)
The dedicated safeguarding code of conduct tailored specifically for Explorer Scout Young Leaders working with younger sections.

### [The Green Card](https://www.scouts.org.uk/volunteers/staying-safe-and-safeguarding/safe-scouting-cards/scouting-and-alcohol/)
The official policy regarding the consumption, management, and presence of alcohol during Scouting activities, trips, and events.

### [Safety Checklist for Trustees](https://www.scouts.org.uk/volunteers/staying-safe-and-safeguarding/safe-scouting-cards/staying-safe-safety-checklist/staying-safe-executive-committees/)
A framework ensuring Trustee Boards cleanly review safety risks, maintain site health and safety compliance, and meet financial governance requirements.

### [Safety Checklist for Section Volunteers](https://www.scouts.org.uk/volunteers/staying-safe-and-safeguarding/safe-scouting-cards/staying-safe-safety-checklist/staying-safe-leaders/)
An essential guide for running section nights, assessing program risk matrices, and keeping regular meeting places safe.

### [Safety Checklist for Lead Volunteers](https://www.scouts.org.uk/volunteers/staying-safe-and-safeguarding/safe-scouting-cards/staying-safe-safety-checklist/staying-safe-managers/)
Operational oversight checklist for Group, District, and County leadership teams managing systemic safety performance.

## Quick links

### [Report a Safeguarding Concern](https://www.scouts.org.uk/volunteers/staying-safe-and-safeguarding/reporting-a-concern-to-safeguarding/)
Direct pathway to flag child protection issues, welfare anomalies, or code of conduct breaches to HQ.

### [What to do in an emergency](https://www.scouts.org.uk/contact-us/#Emergency)
Immediate out-of-hours hotline contacts for duty officers managing critical incidents on the ground.

### [Safety and Safeguarding](https://www.scouts.org.uk/volunteers/staying-safe-and-safeguarding/)
Central hub compiling mandatory templates, risk assessment generators, and protection resources.

### [Equity, Diversity and Inclusion](https://www.scouts.org.uk/volunteers/equity-diversity-and-inclusion/)
Guidance and toolkits ensuring Scouting remains open, accessible, and inclusive to individuals from all backgrounds.

### [Brand Centre](https://scoutsbrand.org.uk/)
Generate logos for your group and section, find images and resources, and print anything you need in the Scout Brand.

### [DBS Checks](https://thescouts.disclosures.co.uk/)
The direct tracking portal for processing and checking background criminal record certifications for adult volunteers.

## Scouting further afield

### [Scouts Scotland](https://www.scouts.scot/)
The autonomous national headquarters supporting local scouting regions across the nation of Scotland.

### [Scouts Cymru](https://scoutscymru.org.uk/)
The dedicated administrative body providing localized training and event infrastructure throughout Wales.

### [Scouts NI](https://www.scoutsni.org/)
The representative regional office providing resources, insurance support, and event plans in Northern Ireland.

### [World Scouting](https://www.scout.org)
The central platform for the World Organization of the Scout Movement (WOSM), connecting global associations.

### [Student Scout & Guide Organisation (SSAGO)](https://ssago.org)
The national support network running active student clubs, social camps, and societies across UK universities and colleges.
