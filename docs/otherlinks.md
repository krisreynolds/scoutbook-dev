## YouTube Channels

### [Big Man In The Woods](https://www.youtube.com/@bigmaninthewoods)
"Big Man in the Woods" is a YouTube channel run by Mark Yates, a Scouter and outdoors enthusiast who shares a variety of Scouting-related content, including gear reviews, campfire songs, and instructional "how-to" guides. The channel serves as a resource for volunteers and young people alike, aiming to promote outdoor skills and the values of the Scouting movement through an engaging and practical lens.

### [Scoutadelic](https://www.youtube.com/@Scoutadelic)
Scoutadelic is a high-energy YouTube channel hosted by Mike Rouse-Deane that focuses on showcasing the fun, modern, and adventurous side of Scouting in the UK and beyond. The channel features a mix of vlogs from major international events, creative programme ideas, and interviews, all delivered with a fast-paced and enthusiastic style designed to appeal to both Scouts and adult volunteers.
