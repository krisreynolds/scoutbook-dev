# Scouting Facebook Groups

## Advice and Ideas

### [1st Facebook Scout Group](https://www.facebook.com/groups/2450689742)
With over 96,000 members from around the world, this is probably the largest group of Scouters you'll find! Members are from different Scout organisations from many different countries. A really useful place to get ideas and connect with others.

### [1st Facebook Beaver Scout Colony](https://www.facebook.com/groups/406372746135055/)
For Beaver Team members around the UK - a place to share ideas for your colony.

### [1st Facebook Cub Scouts Group](https://www.facebook.com/groups/586172824799958/)
For Cub Team members around the UK - a place to share ideas for your pack.

### [1st Facebook Scout Troop](https://www.facebook.com/groups/879047825449791/)
For Scout Team members around the UK - a place to share ideas for your troop.

### [1st Facebook Sea Scout Group](https://www.facebook.com/groups/1stfseascouts/)
A place for all those involved in Sea Scouting in the UK.

### [1st Facebook Scouts - IT Team](https://www.facebook.com/groups/508837139799039/)
Although fairly quiet of late, this is a great place to ask those Scouting IT questions and to find advice from IT experts.

### [Explorer Scout Young Leaders UK](https://www.facebook.com/groups/1942550419372211/)
Questions, advice and guidance for Young Leaders.

### [Gang Shows around the UK](https://www.facebook.com/groups/375189795836517)
Discussions for all things Gang Show!

### [iScoutonstage](https://www.facebook.com/groups/116801038407216)
The Facebook forum for Scout Show production teams to discuss, hang out, share ideas & best practice and most of all to connect!

### [Managers and Trustees in Scouting - The Scouts UK](https://www.facebook.com/groups/475214515844527)
An online space for managers in Scouting to share ideas, best practice and support each other. It is also designed to raise awareness of the leadership and management work as a whole and gives HQ the facility to discuss ideas and issues connected to the leadership and management agenda. It is open to those with a Manager or Trustee role in UK Scouting. Other members of UK Scouting may be permitted to join on a case-by-case basis.

### [Scoutadelic](https://www.facebook.com/scoutadelic/)
An online community for Scoutadelic, an unofficial online Scouting YouTube show like no other. Helped and Contributed by Scouts around the world

### [Scout and Guide Programme Ideas](https://www.facebook.com/groups/403096317215277/)
A group dedicated to sharing programme ideas - share your ideas or ask for help with a topic.

### [Scout Association - Digital Tools Community Support](https://www.facebook.com/groups/798527056875795)
This is the place for the UK Scouting Community to discuss The UK Scout Association New Digital Tools. Stuck with DBS? Want to know how to do something? Need tips and hints? Or are you a Go Live Support team member that can give your wisdom to fellow users ?

### [Scouts UK - Leaders](https://www.facebook.com/groups/scoutsleadersuk/)
Facebook group just for Section Team based Adult Volunteers / Leaders involved with Scouting in the United Kingdom.
an online space for managers in Scouting to share ideas, best practice and support each other. It is also designed to raise awareness of the leadership and management work as a whole and gives HQ the facility to discuss ideas and issues connected to the leadership and management agenda.

### [Scouts UK - OSM - Online Scout Manager](https://www.facebook.com/groups/ukscoutsosm/)
Discuss how to use OSM with other volunteers and share your knowledge!

### [UK Scouts IT Lab](https://www.facebook.com/groups/249968169365755/)
For those in Scouting and IT who like to experiment and try things a little off the wall.

### [UK Scouts on Microsoft365](https://www.facebook.com/groups/1487007118044739/)
A self help group for UK Scouting people who are Implementing or Administering Microsoft365 NFP, EDU or paid licenses to support their organisation

### [UK Scouts on Wordpress](https://www.facebook.com/groups/374069120398215/)
For supporting UK Scout members using WordPress as a website platform

## Buying, Selling and Fundraising

### [1st Facebook Scouts Sales, Services and Fundraising](https://www.facebook.com/groups/1388019144821224/)
This group is the official 1st Facebook Scout Group for all your Scouting memorabilia, used uniform, Scouting Services and Scouting fundraising ideas. A place to share your fundraising items and ideas for up and coming events and jamborees.

### [Scout & Guide Fundraising Group](https://www.facebook.com/groups/414126928657345/)
A group to share ideas and products to raise funds for Scouting and Guiding, as well as selling or exchanging unifom, badges and collectibles.

### [ScoutBay UK](https://www.facebook.com/groups/1498298763796217/)
A place to buy/sell scout related goods, Scouting memorabilia swapping and selling.

## [Scouting Buy, Sell or Swap](https://www.facebook.com/groups/422902374496952)
A group dedicated to the buying, selling or swapping of Scouting items around the world.

### [Scouting Products & Gifts](https://www.facebook.com/groups/233523897004713)
This Group is for anyone to list and link to Scouting products and Gifts for Sale. They can be sold directly from this Group or linked to another website. They can be your own products or they may be something you think is a good Scouting product.

### [UK Scouting Freebies or For Sale](https://www.facebook.com/groups/529920274857957)
This group is for selling or giving away anything that is Scouts branded or can be used for scouting activities. this group is not for selling any items other than Scouts related things such as camping equipment, uniform or anything else a section or leadership team may need.

### [UK Scout Uniform - Buy, sell and swap](https://www.facebook.com/groups/234435900225508/)
This page is for buying, selling and swapping UK Scout uniform.
